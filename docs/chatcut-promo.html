X Unfollow Assistant

Promo brief for a short product video about a Chrome extension that helps users review and clean up their X following list safely.

Chrome Extension Review-first workflow Local-only settings Non-followers mode Built-in cooldowns

What the product does

X Unfollow Assistant is a Chrome extension that runs inside the user’s own logged-in browser session on X and helps review, filter, and remove followed accounts with a safer preview-first workflow.

  • Preview matches before removing anything
  • Review all followed accounts or only people who do not follow back
  • Filter by follower count, follow ratio, account age, activity, avatar type, verified state, and bio words
  • Select specific matched profiles or remove the visible matched set
  • Pause automatically for 10 minutes after every 40 unfollows

Promo direction

Show the product as clean, trustworthy, and X-native rather than spammy or aggressive. The tone should feel premium, minimal, and fast.

  • Dark mode, MacBook-style product shots
  • UI zooms and smooth transitions
  • Highlight safety: preview first, then apply
  • Emphasize non-follower cleanup and targeted filtering

Suggested video flow

  • Open with the extension popup on top of X
  • Show “Preview Matches” to establish the review-first workflow
  • Cut to the results page with avatars and matched accounts
  • Highlight non-followers mode and filter controls
  • Show selective action: remove matched accounts or chosen accounts only
  • Close with trust points: local, review-first, cooldown protected
